Personal Selction Where to go for fun!

While out with friends at boba shops, we understand that over time meeting up and hanging out in the shop gets boring after a while. So we picked out some fun places to visit while out with friends. Some are pretty close, and some are a bit out of the way, but they are definitely worth the commute.

Beautiful sights and tasty drinks! Huntington Garden

This beautiful garden is located in the city of San Marino. It's a botanical garden that is home to many different kinds of plants. It about 130 acres, 16 different themed gardens, and a laboratory for botanical conservation and research.

Distance from Half and Half Tea Express to Huntington Garden is about 2 miles.

From Around the World Atlantic Times Square

Atlantic times square is a small shopping center that is home to a AMC theater, Arcade, and small businesses.

Distance from Tapioca Express to Atlantic Times Square is 1 mile.

Let the good times roll Highland Park Bowl

One of LA's oldest bowling alley that was established in 1927. It has been restored over the years back to its origianl state. The price is steep to bowl but the atmosphere and history definitely make it worth visiting atleast once.

Distance from Magik Milk to Highland Park Bowl is 5 miles.

A nice place to watch the sunset Ernest E. Debs Regional Park

It is a beautiful park to go and walk around through because of the sights you get to see in LA. I believe the best time to go to Debs Park is when the sun is setting because you get to see the sun behind the skyscrapers creating a silhouette of the buildings. Once the sun sets, you get a beautiful night view of LA with all of its lights.

Distance from Its Boba Time to Ernest E. Debs Regional Park is 5 miles.
