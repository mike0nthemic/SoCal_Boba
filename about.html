Boba Milk tea for the masses About Our Web Page

Victor and Michael both have a background in working at boba shops. Victor is a veteran who has worked at many boba shops for about three years. Working at various boba shops gave Victor an understanding of how boba shops compete with one another in providing a great-tasting drink while keeping costs low. Michael served a short term at a boba shop for about six months, where he established a foundation on how boba milk tea drinks are made. Despite having a different experience than Victor, he loves exploring all the various beverages a milk tea shop offers.

We guarantee that you will enjoy our personal selection of drinks and places to visit on our web page.
