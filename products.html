Personal Selction Boba Milk Tea

This is our peronsal selection of boba spots that we think are worth visiting based on our prefrences, dietary needs, and its location.

not milk? Magik Milk

Magik Milk is a unique milk tea shop because all of their drinks are served with oat milk and half sweet. This shop allows everyone to enjoy their favorite milk tea while being healthy!

Address: 12 W Main St Alhambra, CA 91801

What time is it? Its Boba Time

It's Boba Time holds a special place in Michael's heart because it was the first milk tea shop he worked at.

Address: 9 E Main St, Alhambra, CA 91801

Delicious Treats, Good Eats Tapioca Express

Tapioca Express was one of the first few boba milk tea stores to open in the San Gabriel Valley in 1999 and one of the first boba chains in the united states. Soon over the years, this franchise was able to expand across other states like Washington, Texas, and Virginia.

Address: 228 W Valley Blvd STE 105, Alhambra, CA 91801

From Around the World Half & Half Tea Express

Half and Half is a famous Tea spot in southern California because they're known for their hot honey boba drink. They also stood out from the competition because they were the first to use short wide cups for their drinks and became iconic for their store. They've become so popular that I've seen other shops use similar-style cups for their drinks.

Address: 3007 Huntington Drive104, 3007 Huntington Dr #104, Pasadena, CA 91107
